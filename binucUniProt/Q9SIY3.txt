CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the hydrolysis of the beta-gamma-
CC       phosphoanhydride linkage of triphosphate-containing substrates
CC       (inorganic or nucleoside-linked). Catalyzes the hydrolysis of
CC       inorganic triphosphate (PPPi), however it does not display
CC       significant activity towards long-chain polyphosphates. The
CC       existence of PPPi in living cells is still unclear, and PPPase
CC       activity might be the ancestral function of CYTH domain. It also
CC       has gamma-phosphatase activity on NTP substrates, but no adenylate
CC       cyclase or RNA triphosphatase activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:24004165}.
