CC   -!- FUNCTION: One of the primary rRNA binding proteins, this protein
CC       initially binds near the 5'-end of the 23S rRNA. It is important
CC       during the early stages of 50S assembly (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Makes multiple contacts with different domains of the
CC       23S rRNA in the assembled 50S subunit.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Forms part of the polypeptide exit tunnel, in which it
CC       helps forms a bend with protein L22. Contacts the macrolide
CC       antibiotic spiramycin in the polypeptide exit tunnel.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the 50S ribosomal subunit. Interacts weakly with
CC       proteins L18e, L24 and L37e. Has been cross-linked to L18e.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12150912, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12860128}.
