CC   -!- FUNCTION: Located on the upper part of the platform of the 30S
CC       subunit, where it bridges several disparate RNA helices of the 16S
CC       rRNA. Forms part of the Shine-Dalgarno cleft in the 70S ribosome,
CC       where it interacts both with the Shine-Dalgarno helix and mRNA.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the 30S ribosomal subunit. Interacts with
CC       proteins S7 and S18. Binds to the C-terminus of IF-3; however
CC       exactly how IF-3 interacts with the 30S subunit is unclear. May
CC       contact the C-terminus of Era.
