CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endonuclease that specifically degrades the RNA of RNA-
CC       DNA hybrids. RNase H participates in DNA replication; it helps to
CC       specify the origin of genomic replication by suppressing
CC       initiation at origins other than the oriC locus; along with the
CC       5'-3' exonuclease of pol1, it removes RNA primers from the Okazaki
CC       fragments of lagging strand synthesis; and it defines the origin
CC       of replication for ColE1-type plasmids by specific cleavage of an
CC       RNA preprimer.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Endonucleolytic cleavage to 5'-
CC       phosphomonoester.
