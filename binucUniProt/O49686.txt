CC   -!- FUNCTION: Receptor for abscisic acid (ABA) required for ABA-
CC       mediated responses such as stomatal closure and germination
CC       inhibition. Inhibits the activity of group-A protein phosphatases
CC       type 2C (PP2Cs) when activated by ABA (PubMed:19407142,
CC       PubMed:19624469, PubMed:19769575, PubMed:23844015,
CC       PubMed:21658606). Can be activated by both (-)-ABA and (+)-ABA
CC       (PubMed:23844015). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19407142,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19624469, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19769575,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21658606, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23844015}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer
CC       (PubMed:21658606,PubMed:19898494,PubMed:19933100). Binds ABA on
CC       one subunit only. Interacts with HAB1, AHG3, ABI1 and ABI2 when
CC       complexed to ABA, and possibly with other PP2Cs (PubMed:19407142,
CC       PubMed:19874541, PubMed:19898420, PubMed:19898494,
CC       PubMed:19933100). Binds to CARs protein in an ABA-independent
CC       manner, both at the plasma membrane and in the nucleus. Interacts
CC       directly with CAR1 and CAR4 (PubMed:25465408).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19407142, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19874541,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19898420, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19898494,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19933100, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21658606,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:25465408}.
