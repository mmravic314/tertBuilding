CC   -!- FUNCTION: Ribonuclease that specifically degrades pre-mRNAs with a
CC       defective 5' end cap and is part of a pre-mRNA capping quality
CC       control. Has decapping, pyrophosphohydrolase and 5'-3' exonuclease
CC       activities. Has decapping activity toward incomplete 5' end cap
CC       mRNAs such as unmethylated 5' end-capped RNA to release GpppN and
CC       5' end monophosphate RNA. The 5' end monophosphate RNA is then
CC       degraded by the 5'-3' exoribonuclease activity, enabling this
CC       enzyme to decap and degrade incompletely capped mRNAs. Also
CC       possesses RNA 5'-pyrophosphohydrolase activity by hydrolyzing the
CC       5' end triphosphate to release pyrophosphates.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23523372}.
