CC   -!- FUNCTION: This is one of 5 proteins that mediate the attachment of
CC       the 5S rRNA onto the large ribosomal subunit, where it forms part
CC       of the central protuberance and stabilizes the orientation of
CC       adjacent RNA domains.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the 50S ribosomal subunit. Interacts with
CC       proteins L5 and L21e, and attaches the 5S rRNA to the 23S rRNA.
CC       Has been cross-linked to L21e. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12150912,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12860128}.
