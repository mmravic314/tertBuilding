CC   -!- FUNCTION: Virulence effector that plays a role in hijacking the
CC       host vesicular trafficking by recruiting the small guanosine
CC       triphosphatase (GTPase) Rab1 to the cytosolic face of the
CC       Legionella-containing vacuole (LCVs). Acts as an adenosine
CC       monophosphate-protein hydrolase (de-AMPylase) by mediating the
CC       hydrolysis of adenosine 5'-monophosphate (AMP) to 'Tyr-77' of host
CC       RAB1B, thereby releasing RAB1B from bacterial phagosomes and
CC       rendering RAB1B accessible for inactivation by LepB. De-AMPylation
CC       of RAB1B cannot take place when LidA is bound to RAB1B.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21680813, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21734656,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22228731}.
