CC   -!- FUNCTION: Histone methyltransferase. Methylates 'Lys-9' of histone
CC       H3. H3 'Lys-9' methylation represents a specific tag for
CC       epigenetic transcriptional repression. The silencing mechanism via
CC       DNA CpNpG methylation requires the targeting of chromomethylase
CC       CMT3 to methylated histones, probably through an interaction with
CC       an HP1-like adapter. By its function, KYP is directly required for
CC       the maintenance of the DNA CpNpG and asymmetric methylation.
CC       Involved in the silencing of transposable elements.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11898023, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15457214,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15598823, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16277745,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16287862}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: S-adenosyl-L-methionine + L-lysine-[histone] =
CC       S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + N(6)-methyl-L-lysine-[histone].
CC       {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00908}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with H3 histone.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus. Chromosome. Chromosome, centromere.
CC       Note=Associates with centromeric constitutive heterochromatin and
CC       at a lower level with regions of euchromatin.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in leaves stems and flowers.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11691919}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Although the SET domain contains the active site of
CC       enzymatic activity, both pre-SET and post-SET domains are required
CC       for methyltransferase activity.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: In the pre-SET domain, Cys residues bind 3 zinc ions that
CC       are arranged in a triangular cluster; some of these Cys residues
CC       contribute to the binding of two zinc ions within the cluster.
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Mutations in the KYP/SUVH4 gene decrease the level
CC       of histone H3-K9 dimethylated, trimethylated or dimethylated in
CC       association with H3-K14Ac by factors of 4,3 and 3, respectively.
CC       The level of monomethylated H3-K9 is unchanged. Such mutations
CC       lead to a drastic decrease of cytosine methylation at CpNpG sites,
CC       causing the reactivation of endogenous retrotransposons. The
CC       KRYPTONYTE methyltransferase name was given according to its
CC       involvement in SUPERMAN gene silencing.

CC       family. Suvar3-9 subfamily. {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-
