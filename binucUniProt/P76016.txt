CC   -!- FUNCTION: Positively regulates the dhaKLM operon from a sigma-70

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15616579}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: DhaK and DhaL-ADP bind to DhaR in a mutually
CC       exclusive manner, and act as corepressor and coactivator of DhaR,
CC       respectively. DhaK binds to the sensor domain of DhaR and keeps it
CC       in a transcription inactive state. In the presence of
CC       dihydroxyacetone, DhaR activity is activated by DhaL-ADP: DhaK
CC       that binds dihydroxyacetone has less affinity for DhaR and is
CC       displaced by DhaL-ADP, which activates DhaR.
