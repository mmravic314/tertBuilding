CC       diphosphate (FPP) to the bicyclic sesquiterpene aristolochene.
CC       Produces germacrene A as an enzyme-bound intermediate that is not
CC       released by the enzyme, but is further cyclized to produce
CC       aristolochene. Aristolochene is the likely parent compound for a
CC       number of sesquiterpenoid toxins produced by filamentous fungi.
