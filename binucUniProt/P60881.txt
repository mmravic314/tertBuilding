CC   -!- FUNCTION: t-SNARE involved in the molecular regulation of
CC       neurotransmitter release. Modulates the gating characteristics of
CC       the delayed rectifier voltage-dependent potassium channel KCNB1 in
CC       pancreatic beta cells (PubMed:12403834). May play an important
CC       role in the synaptic function of specific neuronal systems.
CC       Associates with proteins involved in vesicle docking and membrane
CC       fusion. Regulates plasma membrane recycling through its
CC       interaction with CENPF. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12403834}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with KCNB1 (via N-terminus); reduces the
CC       voltage-dependent potassium channel KCNB1 activity in pancreatic
CC       beta cells (PubMed:12403834). Interacts with CENP and OTOF. Found
CC       in a complex containing SYT1, SV2B and syntaxin-1. Associates with
CC       the BLOC-1 complex. Isoform 1 and isoform 2 interact with BLOC1S6
CC       (By similarity). Part of the SNARE core complex containing SNAP25,
CC       VAMP2 and STX1A. This complex binds CPLX1. Interacts with TRIM9,
CC       RIMS1, SNAPIN, and HGS. Binds STXBP6. Found in a ternary complex
CC       with STX1A and VAMP8. Interacts with EQTN. Interacts with ZDHHC13
CC       (via ANK repeats). Interacts with ZDHHC17 (via ANK repeats) (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P60879,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12403834}.
