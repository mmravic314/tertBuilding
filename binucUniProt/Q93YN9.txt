CC   -!- FUNCTION: Phosphorylates Ins(1,3,4,5,6)P5 at position 2 to form
CC       Ins(1,2,3,4,5,6)P6 (InsP6 or phytate). Phytate is a regulator of

CC       Ins(1,3,4,6)P4 and Ins(1,4,5,6)P4 to produce Ins(1,2,3,4,6)P5 and
