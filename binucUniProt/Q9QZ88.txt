CC   -!- FUNCTION: Acts as component of the retromer cargo-selective
CC       complex (CSC). The CSC is believed to be the core functional
CC       component of retromer or respective retromer complex variants
CC       acting to prevent missorting of selected transmembrane cargo
CC       proteins into the lysosomal degradation pathway. The recruitment
CC       of the CSC to the endosomal membrane involves RAB7A and SNX3. The
CC       SNX-BAR retromer mediates retrograde transport of cargo proteins
CC       from endosomes to the trans-Golgi network (TGN) and is involved in
CC       endosome-to-plasma membrane transport for cargo protein recycling.
CC       The SNX3-retromer mediates the retrograde endosome-to-TGN
CC       transport of WLS distinct from the SNX-BAR retromer pathway. The
CC       SNX27-retromer is believed to be involved in endosome-to-plasma
CC       membrane trafficking and recycling of a broad spectrum of cargo
CC       proteins. The CSC seems to act as recruitment hub for other
CC       proteins, such as the WASH complex and TBC1D5. Required to
CC       regulate transcytosis of the polymeric immunoglobulin receptor
CC       (pIgR-pIgA) (By similarity). Has no activity towards p-
CC       nitrophenylphosphate, p-nitrophenylphosphorylcholine or
CC       phosphatidylinositlphosphates or a phosphorylated peptide derived
CC       from retromer cargo (in vitro) (PubMed:21629666, PubMed:15965486).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UBQ0, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15965486,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21629666}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the heterotrimeric retromer cargo-selective
CC       complex (CSC), also described as vacuolar protein sorting
CC       subcomplex (VPS) formed by VPS26 (VPS26A or VPS26B), VPS29 and
CC       VPS35 (PubMed:21040701, PubMed:20875039, PubMed:21920005). The CSC
CC       has a highly elongated structure with VPS26 and VPS29 binding
CC       independently at opposite distal ends of VPS35 as central platform
CC       (By similarity). The CSC is believed to associate with variable
CC       sorting nexins to form functionally distinct retromer complex
CC       variants. The originally described retromer complex (also called
CC       SNX-BAR retromer) is a pentamer containing the CSC and a
CC       heterodimeric membrane-deforming subcomplex formed between SNX1 or
CC       SNX2 and SNX5 or SNX6 (also called SNX-BAR subcomplex); the
CC       respective CSC and SNX-BAR subcomplexes associate with low
CC       affinity. The CSC associates with SNX3 to form a SNX3-retromer
CC       complex. The CSC associates with SNX27, the WASH complex and the
CC       SNX-BAR subcomplex to form the SNX27-retromer complex (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with VPS26A, VPS26B, VPS35, ANKRD27
CC       (PubMed:20875039, PubMed:21920005, PubMed:24856514). Interacts
CC       with SNX1, SNX2, SNX27, KIAA0196 (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:Q9UBQ0, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15965486,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20875039, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21040701,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:21920005, ECO:0000269|PubMed:24856514}.
