CC       oxygen-evolving complex of photosystem II. It is not essential for
CC       growth under normal conditions but is required under low CO(2)
CC       concentrations. PSII is a light-driven water plastoquinone
CC       oxidoreductase, using light energy to abstract electrons from
CC       H(2)O, generating a proton gradient subsequently used for ATP
CC       formation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11427679,
