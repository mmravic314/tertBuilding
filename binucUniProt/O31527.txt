CC   -!- FUNCTION: Pectinolytic enzyme that degrades type I
CC       rhamnogalacturonan from plant cell walls and releases disaccharide
CC       products. Degrades rhamnogalacturonan, polygalacturonic acid and
CC       pectic acid. Has very low activity on pectin.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17449691}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Exotype eliminative cleavage of alpha-L-
CC       rhamnopyranosyl-(1->4)-alpha-D-galactopyranosyluronic acid bonds
CC       of rhamnogalacturonan I oligosaccharides containing alpha-L-
CC       rhamnopyranose at the reducing end and 4-deoxy-4,5-unsaturated D-
CC       galactopyranosyluronic acid at the non-reducing end. The products
CC       are the disaccharide 2-O-(4-deoxy-beta-L-threo-hex-4-
CC       enopyranuronosyl)-alpha-L-rhamnopyranose and the shortened
CC       rhamnogalacturonan oligosaccharide containing one 4-deoxy-4,5-
CC       unsaturated D-galactopyranosyluronic acid at the non-reducing end.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:17449691}.
