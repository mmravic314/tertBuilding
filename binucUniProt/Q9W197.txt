CC   -!- FUNCTION: Specifically hydrolyzes 7-methylguanosine monophosphate
CC       (m(7)GMP) to 7-methylguanosine and inorganic phosphate. Also able
CC       to mediate hydrolysis of diphosphate (m(7)GDP) to 7-
CC       methylguanosine and 2 inorganic phosphate with lower activity. The
CC       specific activity for m(7)GMP may protect cells against undesired
CC       salvage of m(7)GMP and its incorporation into nucleic acids. Also
CC       has weak activity for CMP. UMP and purine nucleotides are poor
CC       substrates. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23223233}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: N(7)-methyl-GMP + H(2)O = N(7)-methyl-
CC       guanosine + phosphate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23223233}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: CMP + H(2)O = cytidine + phosphate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23223233}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: A 5'-ribonucleotide + H(2)O = a ribonucleoside
