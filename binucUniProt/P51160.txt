CC       preservation of rod function, as indicated by electroretinogram.
CC       However, some rod involvement may be present in some cone
CC       dystrophies, particularly at late stage. Affected individuals
CC       suffer from photophobia, loss of visual acuity, color vision and
CC       central visual field. Another sign is the absence of macular
CC       lesions for many years. Cone dystrophies are distinguished from
CC       the cone-rod dystrophies in which some loss of peripheral vision
CC       also occurs. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19615668}. Note=The disease is
