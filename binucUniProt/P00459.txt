CC   -!- FUNCTION: The key enzymatic reactions in nitrogen fixation are
CC       catalyzed by the nitrogenase complex, which has 2 components: the
CC       iron protein (component 2) and a component 1 which is either a
CC       molybdenum-iron protein, a vanadium-iron, or an iron-iron protein.

CC       phosphate.
