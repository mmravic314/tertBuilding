CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9620854}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Endonucleolytic cleavage of RNA, removing 5'-
CC       extranucleotides from tRNA precursor.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of nuclear RNase P and RNase MRP complexes.

CC       including POP1, POP3, POP4, POP5, POP6, POP7, POP8, RPP1 and RPR2.
CC       RNase MRP complex consists of an RNA moiety and at least 10
CC       protein subunits including POP1, POP3, POP4, POP5, POP6, POP7,
CC       POP8, RMP1, RPP1 and SNM1, many of which are shared with the RNase
CC       P complex. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15637077,
