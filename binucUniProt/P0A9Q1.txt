CC   -!- FUNCTION: Member of the two-component regulatory system ArcB/ArcA.
CC       Represses a wide variety of aerobic enzymes under anaerobic
CC       conditions. Controls the resistance of E.coli to dyes; required
CC       for expression of the alkaline phosphatase and sex factor F genes;
CC       It also may be involved in the osmoregulation of envelope
CC       proteins. When activated by ArcB, it negatively regulates the
CC       expression of genes of aerobic function. Activates the
CC       transcription of the plfB operon by binding to its promoter.
