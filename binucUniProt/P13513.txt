CC   -!- FUNCTION: TS is a member of the terpene cyclase group of enzymes.
CC       It catalyzes the isomerization and cyclization of farnesyl pyro-
CC       phosphate to form trichodiene, the first cyclic intermediate in
CC       the biosynthetic pathway for trichothecenes. It serves to branch
CC       trichothecene biosynthesis from the isoprenoid pathway.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: (2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate = trichodiene +
CC       diphosphate.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Sesquiterpene biosynthesis; trichothecene biosynthesis.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: Trichothecenes are sesquiterpenoid toxins that act
CC       by inhibiting protein biosynthesis.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the trichodiene synthase family.
