CC       chemoreceptors to the flagellar motors. In its active
CC       (phosphorylated or acetylated) form, CheY exhibits enhanced
CC       binding to a switch component, FliM, at the flagellar motor which
CC       induces a change from counterclockwise to clockwise flagellar
CC       rotation. Overexpression of CheY in association with MotA and MotB
CC       improves motility of a ycgR disruption, suggesting there is an
CC       interaction (direct or indirect) between the c-di-GMP-binding
CC       flagellar brake protein and the flagellar stator.
