CC   -!- DISEASE: Schmid type metaphyseal chondrodysplasia (SMCD)
CC       [MIM:156500]: Dominantly inherited disorder of the osseous
CC       skeleton. The cardinal features of the phenotype are mild short
CC       stature, coxa vara and a waddling gait. Radiography usually shows
CC       sclerosis of the ribs, flaring of the metaphyses, and a wide
CC       irregular growth plate, especially of the knees. A variant form of
CC       SMCD is spondylometaphyseal dysplasia Japanese type. It is
CC       characterized by spinal involvement comprising mild platyspondyly,
CC       vertebral body abnormalities, and end-plate irregularity.
