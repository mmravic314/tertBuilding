CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the spliceosomal U1 snRNP, which is
CC       essential for recognition of the pre-mRNA 5' splice-site and the
CC       subsequent assembly of the spliceosome. U1 snRNP is the first
CC       snRNP to interact with pre-mRNA. This interaction is required for
CC       the subsequent binding of U2 snRNP and the U4/U6/U5 tri-snRNP.
CC       SNRPA binds stem loop II of U1 snRNA. In a snRNP-free form (SF-A)
CC       may be involved in coupled pre-mRNA splicing and polyadenylation
CC       process. May bind preferentially to the 5'-UGCAC-3' motif on RNAs.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9848648}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: U1 snRNP is composed of the 7 core Sm proteins SNRPB,
CC       SNRPD1, SNRPD2, SNRPD3, SNRPE, SNRPF and SNRPG that assemble in an
CC       heptameric protein ring on the Sm site of the small nuclear RNA to
CC       form the core snRNP, and at least three U1 snRNP-specific proteins
CC       SNRNP70/U1-70K, SNRPA/U1-A and SNRPC/U1-C. Interacts with SFPQ;
CC       component of a snRNP-free complex with SFPQ.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:9848648}.
