CC   -!- FUNCTION: May play a role in the regulation of extra-urea cycle
CC       arginine metabolism and also in down-regulation of nitric oxide
CC       synthesis. Extrahepatic arginase functions to regulate L-arginine
CC       bioavailability to NO synthase. Since NO synthase is found in the
CC       penile corpus cavernosum smooth muscle, the clitoral corpus
CC       cavernosum and the vagina, arginase II plays a role in both male
CC       and female sexual arousal. It is therefore a potential target for
CC       the treatment of male and female sexual arousal disorders.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12859189}.
