CC   -!- FUNCTION: Calcium-dependent lectin involved in innate immune
CC       defense. Binds mannose, fucose and N-acetylglucosamine on
CC       different microorganisms and activates the lectin complement
CC       pathway. Binds to late apoptotic cells, as well as to apoptotic
CC       blebs and to necrotic cells, but not to early apoptotic cells,
CC       facilitating their uptake by macrophages (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Oligomeric complex of 3 or more homotrimers.
CC       Oligomerization occurs in the endoplasmic reticulum (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with MASP1 and MASP2. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:10913141}.
