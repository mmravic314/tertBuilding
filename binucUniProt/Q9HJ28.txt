CC       phosphoribosylpyrophosphate (PRPP) onto nicotinate (NA) to yield
CC       nicotinate mononucleotide (NAMN), an intermediate in the
CC       biosynthesis of NAD. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the synthesis of beta-nicotinate D-
CC       ribonucleotide from nicotinate and 5-phospho-D-ribose 1-phosphate
CC       at the expense of ATP. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Nicotinate + 5-phospho-alpha-D-ribose 1-
CC       diphosphate + ATP + H(2)O = beta-nicotinate D-ribonucleotide +
CC       diphosphate + ADP + phosphate.
CC   -!- PATHWAY: Cofactor biosynthesis; NAD(+) biosynthesis; nicotinate D-
CC       ribonucleotide from nicotinate: step 1/1.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer. Forms a trimer of dimers in the crystal.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15753098, ECO:0000269|Ref.3}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: Consists of three domains, an N-terminal domain, a central

CC       zinc knuckle-like motif containing 4 cysteines.

CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the NAPRTase family. Highly divergent.
