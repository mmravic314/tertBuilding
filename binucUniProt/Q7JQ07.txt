CC   -!- FUNCTION: Mediates transposition of transposon Mos1 by a 'cut and
CC       paste' mechanism. Transposases are sequence-specific nucleases and
CC       strand transferases that catalyze transposition through an ordered
CC       series of events: sequence-specific binding of transposase to the
CC       terminal inverted repeats (IR) present at each end of the
CC       transposon, pairing of the transposon IRs in a paired-end complex
CC       (PEC), cleavage of one or both DNA strands at each transposon end,
CC       capture of target DNA, and strand transfer to insert the
CC       transposon at a new site. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19766564}.
