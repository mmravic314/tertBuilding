CC   -!- FUNCTION: NTPase presumably plays a role in replication.
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Viral genome-linked protein is covalently linked to the
CC       5'-end of the positive-strand, negative-strand genomic RNAs and
CC       subgenomic RNA. Acts as a genome-linked replication primer. May
CC       recruit ribosome to viral RNA thereby promoting viral proteins
CC       translation (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: 3C-like protease processes the polyprotein: 3CLpro-RdRp
CC       (p72) is first released by autocleavage, then all other proteins
CC       are cleaved. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: RNA-directed RNA polymerase replicates genomic and
CC       antigenomic RNA by recognizing replications specific signals.
CC       Transcribes also a subgenomic mRNA by initiating RNA synthesis
CC       internally on antigenomic RNA. This sgRNA codes for structural
CC       proteins. Catalyzes the covalent attachment VPg with viral RNAs
CC       (By similarity). {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00539}.
CC   -!- FUNCTION: Capsid protein VP60 self assembles to form an
CC       icosahedral capsid with a T=3 symmetry, about 35 nm in diameter,
CC       and consisting of 180 capsid proteins. A smaller form of capsid
CC       with a diameter of 23 nm might be capsid proteins assembled as
CC       icosahedron with T=1 symmetry. The capsid encapsulate VP2 proteins
CC       and genomic or subgenomic RNA. Attaches virion to target cells by
CC       binding histo-blood group antigens, inducing endocytosis of the
CC       viral particle. Acidification of the endosome induces
CC       conformational change of capsid protein thereby injecting virus
CC       genomic RNA into host cytoplasm (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: NTP + H(2)O = NDP + phosphate.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Endopeptidase with a preference for cleavage
CC       when the P1 position is occupied by Glu-|-Xaa and the P1' position
CC       is occupied by Gly-|-Yaa.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: Nucleoside triphosphate + RNA(n) = diphosphate
CC       + RNA(n+1). {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00539}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds to histo-blood group antigens at surface of target
CC       cells. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Capsid protein VP60: Virion. Host cytoplasm
CC       {ECO:0000250}.
