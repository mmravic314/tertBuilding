CC   -!- FUNCTION: Phosphorelay intermediate protein that is part of the
CC       branched SLN1-YPD1-SKN7/SSK1 two-component regulatory system,
CC       which controls activity of the HOG1 pathway and gene expression in
CC       response to changes in the osmolarity of the extracellular
CC       environment. Catalyzes the phosphoryl group transfer from the
CC       membrane-bound osmosensing histidine kinase SLN1 to two distinct
CC       response regulator proteins, SSK1 in the cytoplasm, and
CC       transcription factor SKN7 in the nucleus.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11073911, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9843501}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with the response regulatory domains of SLN1
CC       and SSK1. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14656441,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8808622}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       P39928:SLN1; NbExp=2; IntAct=EBI-34423, EBI-17357;
CC       Q07084:SSK1; NbExp=3; IntAct=EBI-34423, EBI-18184;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm. Nucleus. Note=Localizes
CC       constitutively to the cytoplasm and the nucleus, independent on
CC       osmotic conditions and phosphorylation status of the protein.
CC   -!- PTM: The phosphorelay mechanism involves the sequential transfer
CC       of a phosphate group from 'His-576' (H1) to 'Asp-1144' (D1) of
CC       SLN1, then to His-64 (H2) of YPD1 and finally to 'Asp-554' (D2) of
CC       SSK1 or 'Asp-427' (D2) of SKN7.
