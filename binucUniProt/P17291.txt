CC   -!- FUNCTION: One of the primary rRNA binding proteins, it binds
CC       directly to 3'-end of the 16S rRNA where it nucleates assembly of
CC       the head domain of the 30S subunit. Is located at the subunit
CC       interface close to the decoding center. Binds mRNA and the E site
CC       tRNA blocking its exit path in the ribosome. This blockage implies
CC       that this section of the ribosome must be able to move to release
CC       the deacetylated tRNA.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Part of the 30S ribosomal subunit. Contacts proteins S9
CC       and S11. Binds to the C-terminus of IF3 and to the C-terminus of
CC       Era.
