CC       Rad50/Mre11 complex possesses single-strand endonuclease activity
CC       and ATP-dependent double-strand-specific 3'-5' exonuclease
CC       activity. Rad50 provides an ATP-dependent control of Mre11 by
CC       unwinding and/or repositioning DNA ends into the Mre11 active
CC       site.
