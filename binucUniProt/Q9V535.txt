CC   -!- FUNCTION: Core component of the splicing-dependent multiprotein
CC       exon junction complex (EJC) deposited at splice junctions on mRNAs
CC       (PubMed:14973490). Involved in exon definition of genes containing
CC       long introns, including the rolled/MAPK gene (PubMed:20946982,
CC       PubMed:20946983). Has a role in oskar mRNA localization to the
CC       posterior pole of the developing oocyte (PubMed:11691839).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11691839, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20946982, ECO:0000269|PubMed:20946983}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Heterodimer (via RRM domain) with mago (PubMed:11691839,
CC       PubMed:12730685, PubMed:12704080). Part of the mRNA splicing-
CC       dependent exon junction complex (EJC) complex; the core complex
CC       contains btz/CASC3, eIF4AIII, mago and tsu/RBM8A
CC       (PubMed:14973490). Interacts with wigb/PYM; the interaction is
CC       direct (PubMed:14968132). Interacts with eIF4AIII
CC       (PubMed:14973490). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11691839,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12704080, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:14968132, ECO:0000269|PubMed:14973490}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       P49028:mago; NbExp=6; IntAct=EBI-172458, EBI-159609;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685}.
CC       Cytoplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685}. Note=Part of the EJC
CC       assembled on mRNAs in the nucleus and remains part of the complex
CC       when mRNA moves into the cytoplasm.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed throughout development. Localizes
CC       to the posterior pole of the oocyte during stages 1-9 of
CC       oogenesis. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11691839}.
CC   -!- DOMAIN: The RNA recognition motif (RRM) is involved in the
CC       interaction with mago. The RNA-binding activity of such domains is
CC       therefore uncertain. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Developing photoreceptor cell clusters
CC       express reduced levels of rolled/MAPK and fail to differentiate
CC       normally. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:20946982,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:20946983}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: 'Tsunagi' means 'connection' or 'link' in Japanese.
CC       {ECO:0000303|PubMed:11691839}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the RBM8A family. {ECO:0000305}.
