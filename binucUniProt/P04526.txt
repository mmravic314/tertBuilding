CC       DNA replication. Required for elongation of primed templates by
CC       controlling the polymerase processivity. Possesses DNA-dependent
CC       ATPase activity on its own and within the heterodimer gp44/gp62.
CC       Progressive binding of ATPs triggers a conformational change in
CC       the complex that inhibits ATPase activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16800623, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18676368}.
