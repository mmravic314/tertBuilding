CC       protection pathways in response to radiation stress. Acts as a
CC       site-specific metalloprotease that cleaves and inactivates the
CC       repressor proteins DdrOC and DdrOP3, resulting in induced
CC       expression of genes required for DNA repair and cell survival
CC       after exposure to radiation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25170972}.
