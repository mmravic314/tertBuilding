CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulation of oxygen dependent gene expression. It
CC       modulates the expression of Iso-1 (CYP1) and Iso-2 (CYP3)
CC       cytochrome c. In response to heme, promotes transcription of genes
CC       encoding functions required for respiration, controlling oxidative
CC       damage and repression of anaerobic genes. Binds to the sequence
CC       5'-CGGNNNTNNCGG-3'. Is non-functional in terms of iso-1 cytochrome
CC       c expression in strain S288c and its derivatives.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10541856, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11689685,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:2851658, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9027731}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds DNA as a homodimer. Interacts with SRO9 and YDJ1.
CC       In the absence of heme, binds to at least four cellular proteins,
CC       including YDJ1 and SRO9, forming a high-molecular-weight complex
CC       (HMC) which results in repression of its activity and dictates its
CC       DNA-binding specificity. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11689685,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8182072, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9886294}.
