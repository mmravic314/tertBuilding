CC   -!- FUNCTION: Displays depupylase (DPUP) activity, removing conjugated
CC       Pup from target proteins; is thus involved in the recycling of Pup
CC       and may function similarly to deubiquitinases (DUBs) in eukaryotes
CC       to prevent or promote proteasomal degradation of certain proteins.
CC       Is also able to catalyze the deamidation of the C-terminal
CC       glutamine to glutamate in a variant of the prokaryotic ubiquitin-
CC       like protein Pup; however, since Pup from A.cellulolyticus
CC       possesses a C-terminal glutamate, this deamidase activity may be
CC       of no significance in vivo. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22910360}.
