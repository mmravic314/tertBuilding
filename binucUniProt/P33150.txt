CC   -!- FUNCTION: Cadherins are calcium-dependent cell adhesion proteins.
CC       They preferentially interact with themselves in a homophilic
CC       manner in connecting cells; cadherins may thus contribute to the
CC       sorting of heterogeneous cell types. May act as a negative
CC       regulator of neural cell growth.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: By contrast to classical cadherins, homodimerization in
CC       trans is not mediated by cadherin EC1 domain strand-swapping, but
CC       instead through a homophilic adhesive interface which joins two
CC       elongated EC1-EC2 domains through a region near their Ca2+-binding
CC       sites to form a tetrahedral, X-like shape.
