CC   -!- FUNCTION: Core component of the splicing-dependent multiprotein
CC       exon junction complex (EJC) deposited at splice junctions on mRNAs
CC       (PubMed:14973490). Involved in exon definition of genes containing
CC       long introns, including the rolled/MAPK gene (PubMed:20946982,
CC       PubMed:20946983). Has a role in oskar mRNA localization to the
CC       posterior pole of the developing oocyte, and may also be involved
CC       in polarization of the oocyte microtubule cytoskeleton
CC       (PubMed:8026338, PubMed:9272960). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:8026338, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9210377,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9272960}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Heterodimer with tsu/RBM8A (PubMed:12704080,
CC       PubMed:12730685, PubMed:14968132). Part of the mRNA splicing-
CC       dependent exon junction complex (EJC) complex; the core complex
CC       contains btz/CASC3, eIF4AIII, mago and tsu/RBM8A
CC       (PubMed:14973490). Interacts with wigb/PYM; the interaction is
CC       direct (PubMed:14968132). Interacts with eIF4AIII
CC       (PubMed:14973490). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12704080,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685, ECO:0000269|PubMed:14968132}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       Q9V535:tsu; NbExp=6; IntAct=EBI-159609, EBI-172458;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685}.
CC       Cytoplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730685}. Note=Part of the EJC
CC       assembled on mRNAs in the nucleus and remains part of the complex
CC       when mRNA moves into the cytoplasm.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed both maternally and zygotically
CC       (PubMed:8026338). Localizes to the posterior pole of the oocyte
CC       during stage 9 of oogenesis (PubMed:14973490).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14973490, ECO:0000269|PubMed:8026338}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: 'Mago nashi' means 'without grandchildren' in
CC       Japanese.
